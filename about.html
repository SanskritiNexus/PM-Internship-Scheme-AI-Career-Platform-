About the PM Internship Scheme

The PM Internship Scheme is a visionary initiative by the Government of India to bridge the gap between education and employment. It aims to provide meaningful internship opportunities to millions of young Indians, helping them gain practical skills, industry exposure, and a pathway to successful careers.

Through this scheme, we connect talented youth with organizations across various sectors, creating a win-win ecosystem that benefits both emerging professionals and the Indian economy.

"The youth of India has unlimited potential. Through the PM Internship Scheme, we are creating pathways for our young talent to contribute to nation-building while developing their own careers. This is not just about jobs; it's about empowering the future of India."

- Shri Narendra Modi, Prime Minister of India

Key Features of the Scheme

The PM Internship Scheme is designed with several innovative features to maximize impact and accessibility for all Indian youth.

Youth Empowerment

Providing opportunities to millions of young Indians to gain practical skills and industry exposure through structured internships.

Nationwide Reach

Ensuring internship opportunities reach every corner of India, including rural and remote areas, through digital accessibility.

Skill Development

Focusing on practical skill enhancement that complements academic knowledge and prepares youth for the demands of the job market.

Industry Partnerships

Collaborating with leading companies, startups, and government organizations to provide quality internship experiences.

AI-Powered Matching

Using advanced technology to match candidates with the most suitable internships based on their skills, interests, and career goals.

Financial Support

Providing stipends and financial assistance to ensure that economic barriers do not prevent talented youth from participating.

Our Vision for India's Youth

The PM Internship Scheme envisions a future where every young Indian has access to quality skill development and career opportunities, regardless of their background or location.
